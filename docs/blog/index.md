title: "Reading the blog"
order: 10
---

What a visitor sees and does on the site: a **home page** that lists every
published post, and a **post page** for reading one article in full. Both reuse
the same article layout, so a post looks the same in the list and on its own
page. Every page is static HTML produced ahead of time, so navigation is
instant and works without a live server.
